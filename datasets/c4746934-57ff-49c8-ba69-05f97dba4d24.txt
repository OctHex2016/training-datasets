Banana Bread Recipe:

Ingredients:
- 3 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 1 tsp baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 4x8 inch loaf pan.
2. In a mixing bowl, combine mashed bananas and melted butter.
3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Then, add the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
4. Gradually add the flour and mix until combined.
5. Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan.
6. Bake for approximately 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
7. Allow the banana bread to cool before serving.
