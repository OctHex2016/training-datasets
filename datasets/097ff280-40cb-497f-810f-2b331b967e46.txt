13. Input: 
car, spare tire, lug wrench, jack
13. Output: 
- Locate the spare tire and lug wrench in the trunk of the car.
- Use the lug wrench to loosen the lug nuts on the flat tire.
- Jack up the car until the flat tire is off the ground.
- Remove the lug nuts and the flat tire, and replace it with the spare tire.
- Tighten the lug nuts, lower the car, and use the lug wrench to tighten the lug nuts again.

19. Instruction: What are the steps to making a grilled cheese sandwich? 
19. Input: 
bread, butter, cheese, pan
19. Output: 
- Heat a pan over medium heat and melt butter.
- Place a slice of bread in the pan and add cheese.
- Top with another slice of bread and butter the top of the bread.
- Flip the sandwich and cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
- Cut the sandwich in half and serve.
