15. Instruction: Describe a recipe for a classic Italian dish like Spaghetti Carbonara and explain each step.
15. Input: 
<noinput>
15. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 1 lb spaghetti
- 4 large eggs
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 cup diced pancetta or bacon
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley or basil, chopped (optional)

Steps:
1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta cooking water.
2. While the pasta is cooking, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the pancetta until crispy. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
4. Remove the pan from the heat and add the drained spaghetti to the pan, tossing to coat with the pancetta and garlic.
5. Pour the egg mixture over the spaghetti, tossing quickly to prevent the eggs from scrambling.
6. Add some of the reserved pasta cooking water to the pan, a little at a time, until the sauce is creamy and coats the spaghetti.
7. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley or basil if desired.
