4. Instruction: Describe a recipe to make guacamole, including ingredients and instructions on what to do. 
4. Input: 
<noinput>
4. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 2 ripe avocados
- 1/2 onion, finely chopped
- 1 jalapeño, minced (remove seeds for less heat)
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tomato, diced
- 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
- 1 lime, juiced
- Salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash the avocado with a fork until your desired texture is achieved. 
2. Add the onion, jalapeño, garlic, tomato, and cilantro to the mashed avocados. Mix well.
3. Pour in the lime juice and add salt to taste, mixing until evenly combined. Adjust seasonings if necessary.
4. Serve immediately or cover with plastic wrap directly touching the surface and store in the refrigerator until needed.
