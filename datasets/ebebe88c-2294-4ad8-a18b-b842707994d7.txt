1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, at room temperature

1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
3 cups all-purpose flour

12. Output:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the unsalted butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
3. Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
4. Stir in vanilla extract.
5. In a separate bowl, whisk together baking soda, salt, and all-purpose flour.
6. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the butter mixture until just combined.
7. Fold in the semisweet chocolate chips.
8. Drop tablespoon-sized rounds of cookie dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
9. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until the edges of the cookies are golden brown.
10. Let cool for 5 minutes on the baking sheet before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

1. Flat tire
2. Spare tire
3. Lug wrench
4. Jack
14. Output:
1. Move the car to a safe, level area away from traffic.
2. Turn on the hazard lights and engage the parking brake.
3. Remove the hubcap or wheel cover with the flat end of the lug wrench.
4. Loosen the lug nuts on the flat tire, but do not remove them yet.
5. Place the jack under the car frame near the flat tire and raise the car until the tire is off the ground.
6. Remove the lug nuts and the flat tire, and place them aside.
7. Position the spare tire onto the wheel studs and replace the lug nuts.
8. Lower the car until it is resting on the ground, and remove the jack.
9. Tighten the lug nuts in a star pattern using the lug wrench.
10. Replace the wheel cover or hubcap.
11. Check the tire pressure on the spare tire and inflate if necessary.
(Instructions for changing a flat tire on a car.)
