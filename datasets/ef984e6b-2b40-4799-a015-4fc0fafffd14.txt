Ingredients:
- 1 ripe avocado
- 2 slices of whole-grain bread
- 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
- Sea salt, to taste
- Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Portion size: Serves 1

Preparation steps:
1. Gather all necessary ingredients.
2. Toast the slices of bread to your desired level of crispness.
3. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
4. Add the lemon juice and a pinch of sea salt to the bowl.
5. Mash the avocado with a fork until it reaches your desired consistency (either smooth or slightly chunky).
6. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto each slice of toasted bread.
7. Sprinkle with additional sea salt and crushed red pepper flakes (if desired).
8. Enjoy your delicious avocado toast!
