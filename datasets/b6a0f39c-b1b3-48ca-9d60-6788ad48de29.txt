To make a chocolate cake from scratch, you'll need:
- 2 cups of sugar
- 1 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
- 3/4 of a cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking soda
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup of milk
- 1/2 of a cup of vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
- 1 cup of boiling water

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175 °C). 
2. Grease and flour two 9-inch (23 cm) cake pans.
3. Combine sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large mixing bowl. 
4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed for 2 minutes. 
5. Stir in boiling water (the batter will be thin). Pour batter evenly into prepared pans.
6. Bake 30 to 25 minutes in the oven, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
7. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans. Let them cool down completely on a wire rack.
