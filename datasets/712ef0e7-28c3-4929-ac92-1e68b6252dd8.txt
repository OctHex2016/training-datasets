10. Instruction: Summarize the key differences between communism and capitalism.
10. Input: <noinput>
10. Output: 
Communism emphasizes the collective ownership of resources and the equal distribution of wealth, with the state controlling production and distribution. Capitalism, on the other hand, is built on private ownership, free markets, and personal profit. While communism aims for a classless society with minimal income inequality, capitalism encourages wealth accumulation and results in natural social stratification.
