20. Instruction: Briefly explain the economic concept of "supply and demand."
20. Input: 
<noinput>
20. Output: 
Supply and demand is an economic concept that governs the prices and quantities of goods and services in a market. Demand refers to the consumer willingness to purchase a particular product or service at a specific price, while supply is the quantity of that product or service that producers are willing to offer at various price levels. The interaction between supply and demand determines the market price and quantity of goods and services in equilibrium. When demand exceeds supply, prices typically increase, and when supply exceeds demand, prices typically decrease.
