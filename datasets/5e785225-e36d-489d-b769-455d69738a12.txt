20. Output: 
The greenhouse effect occurs when certain gases in Earth's atmosphere, such as carbon dioxide and methane, trap heat from the Sun. These gases act like a blanket, keeping the planet's temperature warmer than it would be without them. While some heat is necessary to maintain a habitable climate, excess greenhouse gases can lead to global warming and climate change.
