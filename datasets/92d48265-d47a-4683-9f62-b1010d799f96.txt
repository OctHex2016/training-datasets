4. Instruction: Tell me how to bake a chocolate cake. 
4. Input: 
(noinput)
4. Output: 
Ingredients: 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp baking soda, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 cup vegetable oil, 1 1/4 cups sugar, 2 large eggs, 2 tsp vanilla extract, 1 cup milk. 
Instructions: 
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
2. Grease 2 9-inch round cake pans
3. Whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl
4. In a separate large bowl, whisk together vegetable oil, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until smooth
5. Gradually mix in dry ingredients and milk, alternatively, stirring after each addition until batter is smooth
6. Divide batter evenly between the prepared cake pans and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean
7. Cool cakes completely on a wire rack before frosting
