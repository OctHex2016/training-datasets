13. Instruction: What is the recipe to make chocolate chip cookies? 
13. Input: 
1/2 cup salted butter, at room temperature  
1/2 cup white (granulated) sugar  
1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed  
1 large egg  
1 teaspoon vanilla extract  
1/2 teaspoon baking soda  
1/2 teaspoon salt  
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour  
1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips  
13. Output: 
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the egg and vanilla.
3. In a small bowl, mix together the baking soda and salt. Add to the large bowl and mix in.
4. Mix in the flour.
5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
6. Scoop out rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets.
7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
8. Cool completely before serving.
