Sunset colors occur due to a phenomenon called Rayleigh scattering. As the sun gets lower in the sky, its light passes through a thicker layer of the Earth's atmosphere. The atmosphere filters out shorter wavelengths, like blue and green, scattering them away from our line of sight.
