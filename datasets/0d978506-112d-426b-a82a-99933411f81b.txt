15. Instruction: Generate a list of ingredients and directions for a vegetarian pasta dish. 
15. Input: 
<noinput>
15. Output:
Ingredients:
- 12 ounces pasta 
- 2 tablespoons olive oil 
- 1 small onion, diced 
- 3 cloves garlic, minced 
- 1 small jar sun-dried tomatoes, drained and chopped 
- 1 can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped 
- 1/4 cup kalamata olives, pitted and chopped 
- 1/2 cup vegetable broth 
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 
- Fresh basil, chopped 
Directions:
1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
2. In a separate pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté for about 3 minutes, until fragrant.
3. Add the sun-dried tomatoes, artichoke hearts, and olives to the pan and stir well.
4. Pour in the vegetable broth and let the mixture simmer for about 5 minutes.
5. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to combine.
6. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil and serve.
