Chess is a strategic board game played between two players. Each player starts with 16 pieces: 1 king, 1 queen, 2 rooks, 2 knights, 2 bishops, and 8 pawns. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king, meaning it is in threat of capture and has no legal move to escape the threat. Players take turns moving one piece at a time, with each piece having specific movement rules. 16. Instruction: Provide a recipe for making vegan chocolate chip cookies.
16. Input:
<noinput>
16. Output:
Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup coconut oil (melted)
- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup non-dairy milk (e.g., almond or soy milk)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, mix melted coconut oil, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until well combined.
3. Stir in non-dairy milk and vanilla extract.
4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
5. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, mixing until combined.
6. Fold in vegan chocolate chips.
7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto prepared baking sheet, leaving space between cookies.
8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.
9. Remove from oven, allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
