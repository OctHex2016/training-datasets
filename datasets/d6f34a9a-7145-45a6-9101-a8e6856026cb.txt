- 1 tsp baking soda

- 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
2. Mix the flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl and set aside.

11. Output: 

Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. In a bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, and salt.
3. Beat the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar in a large mixing bowl until smooth.
4. Beat in the eggs and vanilla extract until smooth.
5. Gradually stir in the flour mixture.
6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
7. Place spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheet.
8. Bake for 8-10 minutes until light brown.
