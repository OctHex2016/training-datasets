20. Instruction: Write a recipe for a dish with the given ingredient. 
20. Input: 
Ingredient: potatoes
20. Output: 
Crispy Garlic Roasted Potatoes

Ingredients:
- 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp paprika

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. In a large bowl, combine diced potatoes, minced garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, and paprika.
3. Toss until well coated.
4. Spread potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet.
5. Roast for 25-30 minutes, until crispy and golden brown.
