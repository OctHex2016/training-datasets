Ingredients:
- 8 oz spaghetti noodles
- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp hot sauce
- 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup chopped scallions
- 1/4 cup chopped peanuts

Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. While spaghetti cooks, combine peanut butter, soy sauce, garlic, and hot sauce in a small bowl.
3. Once spaghetti is cooked, drain and return to pot.
4. Toss with peanut sauce until well coated.
5. Add sliced red pepper and mix well.
6. Divide into bowls and top with chopped scallions and peanuts. Enjoy!
