12. Instruction: What are the steps to change a tire on a car?
12. Input: 
<noinput>
12. Output: 
1. Find a safe location off the road and turn on hazard lights.
2. Apply the parking brake and place a wheel chock behind the opposite wheel.
3. Retrieve the jack, lug wrench, and spare tire from the trunk.
4. Loosen the lug nuts on the flat tire using the lug wrench.
5. Use the jack to lift the car until the flat tire is no longer in contact with the ground.
6. Remove the lug nuts and flat tire.
7. Mount the spare tire and replace the lug nuts.
8. Use the lug wrench to tighten the lug nuts as much as possible.
9. Slowly lower the car back down to the ground.
10. Use a torque wrench to fully tighten the lug nuts to the manufacturer's specifications.
