Ingredients:

- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 bell pepper, chopped
- 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 can (10 oz) diced tomatoes and green chilies
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 bell pepper, chopped

- 1 can (10 oz) diced tomatoes and green chilies

- 1 tsp cumin
