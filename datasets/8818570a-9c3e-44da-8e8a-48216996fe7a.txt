6. Instruction: Please find a recipe for sugar-free chocolate chip cookies. 
6. Input: 
<noinput>
6. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp. baking soda
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 3/4 cup granulated erythritol
- 3/4 cup brown sugar substitute (such as Lakanto Monkfruit)
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tsp. pure vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups sugar-free chocolate chips (such as Lily's)
Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
3. In a large bowl, beat butter, erythritol, and brown sugar substitute together until light and fluffy.
4. Add eggs and vanilla extract and mix until well combined.
5. Gradually stir in flour mixture, then fold in chocolate chips.
6. Using a cookie scoop, drop dough onto prepared baking sheet.
7. Bake until edges are lightly golden, about 12-15 minutes. Allow cookies to cool for 5 minutes before transferring to wire rack to cool completely.

<noinput>
12. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 2 large eggs
- 2 tbsp. of butter
- Splash of milk
- Salt
- Pepper
Instructions:
1. Crack eggs into a bowl and add a splash of milk. Beat until well combined.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Melt butter in a nonstick pan over medium-low heat.
4. Once the butter has melted, pour the egg mixture into the pan.
5. Gently scramble the eggs with a spatula, lifting and folding the eggs from the edges.
6. Cook until the eggs are just set but still soft and a little runny.
7. Serve hot and enjoy!
