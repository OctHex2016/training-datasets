Ingredients:
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 carrot, grated
- 500g lean minced beef
- 400g canned crushed tomatoes
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- Salt and black pepper
- 350g spaghetti
- Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)
- Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and grated carrot. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened.
2. Add the minced beef to the pan and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spatula as it cooks.
3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, and Italian seasoning. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 25-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
5. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves, if desired. Enjoy your delicious and easy spaghetti Bolognese!
