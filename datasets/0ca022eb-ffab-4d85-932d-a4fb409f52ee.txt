Garlic-Parsley Toast Recipe:

Ingredients:
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 loaf of crusty bread, sliced in half horizontally

Directions:
1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a small mixing bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, and chopped parsley until well-blended.
3. Spread the garlic-parsley butter mixture evenly over both halves of the sliced bread.
4. Place the bread halves, buttered side up, on a baking sheet.
5. Bake for 10 minutes in the preheated oven or until the bread is golden and crispy.
6. Cut the baked toast into smaller pieces and serve warm. Enjoy it as a delicious appetizer or side dish.
