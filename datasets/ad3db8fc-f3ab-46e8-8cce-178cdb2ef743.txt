Ingredients:
· 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
· 1 tsp sugar
· 1 tsp salt
· 1 package instant yeast
· 1 ½ cups warm water
· 3 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt.
2. Add yeast and water to the mixture and stir.
3. Knead the dough for 5-10 mins till it becomes smooth.
4. Grease another bowl with olive oil and place the dough in it.
5. Cover the bowl with a kitchen towel and let the dough rise for an hour.
6. Once the dough is ready, punch it down and knead it for a few more minutes.
7. Flatten and roll the dough to your desired thickness.
Enjoy your homemade pizza!
