Chicken Spinach and Tomato Skillet

Ingredients:
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 2 cups fresh spinach
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup basil, chopped
- 1/4 cup white wine
- 1/4 cup chicken broth
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:
1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
2. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat.
3. Cook chicken breasts for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Remove from skillet and set aside.
4. In the same skillet, add the other tablespoon of olive oil and saut√© the garlic for 30 seconds.
5. Add the cherry tomatoes, basil, white wine, and chicken broth to the skillet. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tomato skins start to burst.
6. Add the spinach to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, until wilted.
7. Return the cooked chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for an additional 2 minutes, allowing flavors to blend.
8. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top of the chicken and cook until the cheese begins to melt.
9. Serve hot with a side dish of your choice (rice, pasta or mixed greens).
