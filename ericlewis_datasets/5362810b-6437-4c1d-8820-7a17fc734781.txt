Ingredients:
- 3 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional fillings such as cheese, ham, mushrooms 
Cooking Steps:
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk until well combined. Add salt and pepper to taste. 
2. Heat the butter in a non-stick pan on medium to high heat. When pan is heated, pour in the egg mixture. 
3. Stir gently with a spatula until desired doneness is achieved.
4. Add any optional fillings to one side of the omelette if desired. 
5. Using a spatula, fold one side of the omelette onto the other side. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately. Enjoy!
