20. Output: 
Solar panels generate electricity through the photovoltaic (PV) effect. They contain semiconductor cells made of materials like silicon, which absorb sunlight. When photons from the sun hit the solar cells, they excite the electrons within the semiconductor material, causing them to flow and generate an electric current. This direct current (DC) is then converted into alternating current (AC) by an inverter for use in homes and businesses.
