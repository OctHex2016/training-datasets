18. Instruction: Provide a recipe for a simple dinner dish. 
18. Input: 
Dish: Spaghetti with tomato sauce
18. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 1 lb spaghetti 
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (28 oz) can of crushed tomatoes
- salt and pepper to taste
- grated Parmesan cheese for topping

Instructions:
1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta water before draining.
2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
3. Add the crushed tomatoes and salt and pepper to taste. Increase the heat to high and bring to a simmer.
4. Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes.
5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the tomato sauce and toss to coat. If the sauce seems too thick, add some of the reserved pasta water.
6. Divide the spaghetti onto plates and top each serving with grated Parmesan cheese. Enjoy!
