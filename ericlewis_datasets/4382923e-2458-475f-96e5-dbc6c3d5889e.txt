18. Instruction: Write a short recipe to prepare a simple tomato soup.
18. Input: 
<noinput>
18. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. In a large pot, cook the chopped onion in a little cooking oil over medium heat until translucent.
2. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
3. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the tomatoes are soft.
4. Remove from heat and let the soup cool down slightly. Blend the soup using a hand blender or a countertop blender until smooth.
5. Stir in the chopped fresh basil and season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
