10. Instruction: Given the list of ingredients, write down the steps to make a lasagna.
10. Input: 
1 pound sweet Italian sausage 
3/4 pound lean ground beef 
1/2 cup minced onion 
2 cloves garlic, crushed 
1 can crushed tomatoes 
2 cans tomato paste 
2 cans tomato sauce 
1/2 cup water 
2 tablespoons white sugar 
1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves 
1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds 
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning 
1 tablespoon salt 
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper 
4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley 
12 lasagna noodles 
16 ounces ricotta cheese
1 egg 
1/2 teaspoon salt 
3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced 
3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
10. Output: 
1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. In a large pot, cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic until well browned.
3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. 
4. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. 
5. Simmer for about 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
6. In a large bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
7. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. 
8. Drain and rinse in cold water.
9. In a 9×13″ baking dish, arrange a layer of noodles, then layer of ricotta mixture, then mozzarella cheese, and then top with sauce.
10. Repeat layers, ending with parmesan cheese on top.
11. Bake uncovered for 25 minutes.
12. Let the lasagna sit for at least 10 minutes before serving.
