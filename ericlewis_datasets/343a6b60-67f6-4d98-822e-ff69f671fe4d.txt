Spaghetti with Meat Sauce:
1. Heat a large saucepan over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
2. Add one minced garlic clove and one diced medium onion to the pan and cook until onions are translucent.
3. Add 1 pound of ground beef to the pan, breaking it up with a wooden spoon. Cook until browned.
