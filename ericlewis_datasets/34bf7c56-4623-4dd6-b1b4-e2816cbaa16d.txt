8. Instruction: Please provide step by step instructions for cooking fried rice. 
8. Input:
2 cups leftover white rice

1/2 onion, chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

1/4 cup peas

1/4 cup carrots, chopped

1 egg

1 tbsp. oil

Soy sauce and black pepper to taste.

8. Output:
1. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onion and garlic. Stir until fragrant.

2. Add chopped carrots and peas to the pan. Fry for a few minutes until cooked.

3. Push the vegetables to the edge of the pan, scramble the egg in the middle. Wait until the egg is done before mixing it with the vegetables.

4. Add leftover rice to the pan, fry with the egg and vegetables until everything is heated through.

5. Season with soy sauce and black pepper to taste.
