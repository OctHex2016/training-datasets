5. Instruction: Suggest a recipe for a vegan gluten-free lasagna.
5. Input: 
- 1 box gluten-free lasagna noodles.
- 1 jar marinara sauce
- 1 package vegan ground beef crumbles
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and excess water squeezed out
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
- 1 carton vegan ricotta cheese
- 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
- Salt and pepper
5. Output: 
Preheat oven to 350 F. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and sauté until onions are translucent (about 5 minutes). Add vegan ground beef crumbles and continue to sauté until heated through. Add spinach and marinara sauce to skillet and stir until well mixed. Remove from heat. In a separate bowl, mix vegan ricotta cheese, fresh basil, nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper. In a baking dish, layer cooked noodles, sauce mixture, and ricotta mixture, and repeat until all ingredients are used up. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.
