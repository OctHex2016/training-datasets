Recipe for Vegetarian Lasagna:

Ingredients:
- 1 box of lasagna noodles
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 zucchini, sliced
- 1 eggplant, sliced
- 1 can of diced tomatoes
- 1 cup of tomato sauce
- 1 cup of ricotta cheese
- 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375°F
2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package directions and set aside.
3. In a large skillet over medium heat, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
4. Add the zucchini and eggplant, and cook until soft.
5. Stir in the diced tomatoes and tomato sauce.
6. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and one cup of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
7. Spread a layer of the tomato sauce mixture on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
8. Layer the cooked noodles on top, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture.
9. Repeat these layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of tomato sauce on top.
10. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top of the lasagna.
11. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and brown.
13. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
