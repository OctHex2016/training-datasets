Ingredients: 
1 cup heavy cream
12 ounces of chocolate chips
2 tablespoons raspberry extract
1 tablespoon orange extract
1 teaspoon of salt
1 cup cocoa powder
Directions: 
1. In a small bowl, combine the heavy cream, salt and raspberry and orange extract.  
2. Heat the mixture over low heat just until it starts to steam. Do not let the mixture boil.  
3. Remove the mixture from the heat and pour the chocolate chips into the bowl.

7. Roll each truffle into a ball then roll them in cocoa powder to coat them. 
8. Serve immediately or refrigerate.
