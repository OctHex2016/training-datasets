4. Instruction: Write the recipe for a simple spaghetti aglio e olio.
4. Input: <noinput>
4. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 400g spaghetti
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti according to the package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water. Drain, reserving 1 cup of the pasta water.
2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat, then add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, cooking for about 1 minute or until fragrant.
3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan, along with a little pasta water as needed, stirring to coat the pasta with the garlic oil. Season with salt and pepper and cook for 2-3 minutes.
4. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh parsley. Serve immediately.
