14. Instruction: Explain how to make a simple French omelette in a few steps.

14. Input:
<noinput>
14. Output:
1. Gather ingredients: 2-3 eggs, salt, pepper, butter, and optional fillings such as cheese, vegetables, or herbs.
2. Beat the eggs in a bowl, adding a pinch of salt and pepper for seasoning.
3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a small amount of butter, allowing it to melt and coat the pan evenly.
4. Pour the beaten egg mixture into the pan and let it cook undisturbed for a few seconds.
5. Gently stir and lift the cooked edges of the omelette using a spatula, allowing the uncooked egg to flow underneath.
6. Once the omelette is just set with a slightly moist surface, add any desired fillings to one side of the omelette.
7. Carefully fold the omelette in half with the spatula, covering the fillings.
8. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately.

Enjoy your simple French omelette!
