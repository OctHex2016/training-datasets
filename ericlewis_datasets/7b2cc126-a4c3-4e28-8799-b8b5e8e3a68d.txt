13. Instruction: Generate a recipe for a vegetarian lasagna.
13. Input:
<noinput>
13. Output:
Ingredients:
- 1 box of lasagna noodles
- 4 cups of tomato sauce
- 1 1/2 cups of ricotta cheese
- 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese
- 2 cups of spinach leaves
- 2 cups of mushroom slices
- 1 minced garlic clove

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven at 350F.
2. Cook noodles according to the package and set aside.
3. In a skillet, sauté mushrooms and garlic until golden, add spinach and sauté for 3 more minutes.
4. In a large bowl, mix ricotta cheese and 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese.
5. Spread a layer of tomato sauce in a baking dish, cover with a layer of noodles, then a layer of ricotta cheese mixture and a layer of the mushroom and spinach mix.
6. Repeat step 5 until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of tomato sauce.
7. Sprinkle remaining parmesan cheese on top.
8. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes.
