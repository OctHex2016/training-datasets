10. Input: <noinput>

Compound Interest = P(1 + r/n)^(nt) - P

P = Principal amount ($5,000)
r = Annual interest rate (0.06)
t = Time period (5 years)
n = Number of times interest is compounded per year (1)

Compound Interest = 5000(1 + 0.06/1)^(1*5) - 5000
Compound Interest = 5000(1.06)^5 - 5000
Compound Interest = $1,689.48
