8. Instruction: Describe the basic steps to make tomato soup from scratch.
8. Input:
<noinput>
8. Output:
1. Gather ingredients: tomatoes, onion, garlic, vegetable broth, olive oil, salt, pepper, and optionally, basil or other herbs.
2. Dice onions and mince garlic.
3. In a large pot, heat olive oil, then sauté onions and garlic until softened.
4. Chop tomatoes and add them to the pot. Cook for a few minutes, allowing the tomatoes to release their juices.
5. Add the vegetable broth to the pot and bring to a boil. Lower heat and let it simmer for 20-30 minutes.
6. If using, add chopped basil or herbs of choice.
7. Puree the soup using a blender, immersion blender, or food processor until smooth.
8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Once heated through, serve the soup either hot or cold.
