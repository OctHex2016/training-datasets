20. Instruction: List the ingredients and steps to bake a chocolate cake from scratch.
20. Input: 
<noinput>
20. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup boiling water

Steps:
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
3. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla and mix with an electric mixer until well combined.
4. Stir in boiling water last, and blend until smooth.
5. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
6. Bake for 30-35 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
7. Cool the cake layers in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on wire racks.
8. Frost and decorate as desired.
