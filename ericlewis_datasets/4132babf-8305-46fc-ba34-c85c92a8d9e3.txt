13. Instruction: Explain the basic rules of playing Uno.
13. Input: 
<noinput>
13. Output: 
In Uno, the objective is to be the first player to get rid of all your cards. Players take turns matching a card in their hand with the card shown on top of the discard pile based on either color, number, or symbol. If a player cannot play a card, they must draw a card from the draw pile. Special action cards (Skip, Reverse, Draw Two, Wild, and Wild Draw Four) can alter the game. When a player has only one card left, they must shout "Uno!" to avoid penalties. The game continues until a player has no cards left.
