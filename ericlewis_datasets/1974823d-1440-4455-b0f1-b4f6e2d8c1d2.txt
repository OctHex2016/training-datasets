Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe 
Ingredients:
- 8 oz spaghetti
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
- 4 slices bacon, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- Salt, to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together egg yolks and parmesan cheese until well combined. Set aside.
3. In a large skillet, cook bacon over medium heat until crispy. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
4. Remove skillet from heat and add cooked spaghetti to the pan, tossing to coat with the bacon and garlic juices.
5. Add egg yolk mixture to the pan and toss until the spaghetti is coated with the sauce, about 1-2 minutes.
6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste, and serve immediately. Enjoy!
