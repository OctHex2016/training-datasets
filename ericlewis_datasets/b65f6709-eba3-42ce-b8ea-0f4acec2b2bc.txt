15. Instruction: List the ingredients and instructions for making vegetable stir-fry.
15. Input: 
<noinput>
15. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 bell peppers, thinly sliced
- 2 zucchini, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon honey
Instructions:
1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the garlic and onion and cook until the onion is translucent.
3. Add in the bell peppers and zucchini and cook until they become tender, about 5 minutes.
4. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, cornstarch, and honey.
5. Pour the sauce into the skillet and stir until everything is well-coated.
6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened.
Serve over rice or noodles.
