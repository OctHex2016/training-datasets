Ingredients:
- 1 pound of fettuccine pasta

- 2 cups of heavy cream
- 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper
- 4 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons of butter
- Parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

2. Season chicken with salt and pepper
3. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium-high heat

7. Add grated Parmesan cheese and mix until fully melted
8. Drain pasta and add it to the sauce
9. Toss pasta until fully coated
10. Garnish with parsley and additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.
