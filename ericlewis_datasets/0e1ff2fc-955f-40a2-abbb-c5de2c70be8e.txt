20. Output: 
The greenhouse effect is a natural process that occurs when certain gases in Earth's atmosphere, such as carbon dioxide, water vapor, and methane, trap heat from the sun, preventing it from escaping back into space. This trapped heat warms the Earth's surface, maintaining a habitable temperature for life. However, increased levels of greenhouse gases due to human activities can exacerbate the greenhouse effect, leading to global warming and climate change.
