7. Instruction: Write a recipe for your favorite dish. 
7. Input: 
Mushroom risotto with parmesan and truffle oil. 
7. Output: 
Mushroom Risotto:
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1/2 cup chopped onion
- 2 cups Arborio rice
- 1/2 cup dry white wine
- 4 cups sliced mushrooms
- 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper
- Truffle oil (optional)
In a medium-sized pot, bring the vegetable broth to a simmer. In a separate large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil and sauté the chopped onion until translucent. Add the Arborio rice and sauté until the rice grains begin to turn translucent, about 2-3 minutes. Add the white wine to the rice and cook until the wine has been absorbed by the rice.
Begin adding the hot vegetable broth to the rice, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly and waiting until each ladleful is absorbed before adding the next. Cook the rice until it's al dente, and then add the sliced mushrooms, grated Parmesan cheese, butter and salt and pepper to taste. Serve with a drizzle of truffle oil (optional) on top. Enjoy!
