11. Instruction: Write a recipe for homemade pizza dough. 
11. Input: 
2 1/4 teaspoons of active dry yeast, 1/2 teaspoon of sugar, 1 1/2 cups of warm water, 1 tablespoon of salt, 3 tablespoons of olive oil, and 3 1/3 cups of all-purpose flour.
11. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 2 1/4 teaspoons of active dry yeast
- 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
- 1 1/2 cups of warm water
- 1 tablespoon of salt
- 3 tablespoons of olive oil
- 3 1/3 cups of all-purpose flour

Instructions:
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let sit for 5 minutes until the mixture becomes bubbly.
2. Add the salt, olive oil, and flour to the bowl. Mix until the dough forms a smooth, elastic ball.
3. Knead the dough for 5-10 minutes on a floured surface until it becomes smooth and pliable.
4. Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl and cover with a towel. Let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.
5. Punch down the dough and roll it out into your desired shape.
6. Add your favorite toppings and bake in a preheated oven at 450 degrees F for 20-25 minutes. Enjoy!
