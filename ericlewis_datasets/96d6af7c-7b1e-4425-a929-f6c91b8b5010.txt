Ingredients:
- 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 2 tbsp granulated sugar
- 1/8 tsp baking powder
- 1 pinch of salt
- 3 tbsp milk
- 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
- 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract

Instructions:
1. In a microwave-safe mug, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
2. Add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract to the mug. Stir until fully combined, making sure there are no clumps of dry ingredients.
3. Microwave the mug cake for 70-90 seconds on high, or until the cake has risen and is set in the middle. Adjust the time depending on the microwave wattage.
4. Allow the mug cake to cool slightly, then enjoy!
