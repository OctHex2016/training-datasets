4. Instruction: Convert the following recipe to a vegan alternative.
4. Input: 
Ingredients:
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup whole milk
- 2 large eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, milk, eggs, and salt until smooth.
2. Stir in the melted butter.
3. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and pour in 1/4 cup of the batter.
4. Cook the pancake for 2 minutes on one side until golden, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
5. Repeat with the remaining batter, and serve warm with your favorite toppings.
4. Output: 
Ingredients:
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup almond milk (or other plant-based milk)
- 2 tablespoons flaxseed meal mixed with 6 tablespoons water (egg alternative)
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon vegan butter, melted

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, almond milk, flaxseed meal mixture, and salt until smooth.
2. Stir in the melted vegan butter.
3. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and pour in 1/4 cup of the batter.
4. Cook the pancake for 2 minutes on one side until golden, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
5. Repeat with the remaining batter, and serve warm with your favorite vegan toppings.
